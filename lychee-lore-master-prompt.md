# MASTER PROMPT — LYCHEE LORE (paste into Lovable)

Act as an Awwwards Site of the Year UX/UI designer and elite frontend developer with 25+ years of luxury e-commerce experience. Build a complete, **mobile-first** premium e-commerce frontend for a vintage jewellery brand called **"lychee lore"** — a boutique selling one-of-a-kind vintage pieces in limited "drops".

**The Goal:** Do NOT build a standard e-commerce template. Build an immersive emotional journey that feels like a breathtaking, candlelit vintage collage. The aesthetic must perfectly replicate a moody, maximalist dark academia / romantic vintage desk scene at twilight. 

**Visual north star:** A deeply layered, rich, and cluttered (in a beautiful way) collage.
- **The Backdrop:** A large arched window looking out onto a dusk-purple starry sky with the silhouette of an old European city.
- **The Desk Elements:** Melting white wax candles, a classic alabaster bust statue resting her head, a vintage camera, and a gold pocket watch.
- **The Florals:** Deep mauve, dusty rose, and dark purple flowers scattered and in glass vases. Purple butterflies resting on objects.
- **The Paper & Words:** Torn pieces of aged parchment pinned up with typewriter poetry ("maybe we are the poets the universe wrote to understand itself. ♡"). An open journal with pressed botanical flowers and cursive handwriting ("I wish I met me in another life. ♡"). Dark cards with gold celestial text ("CHASE THE MOON not the people.").
- **The Light:** A glass jar overflowing with glowing fairy lights, casting warm amber reflections across the dark wood. A white coffee cup reading "romanticize your ordinary days."

NEVER spooky, never horror-gothic, never glossy, never sterile. It must feel deeply human, literary, and overflowing with romantic melancholy.

---

## CRITICAL RULES

1. **Mobile-first.** Design every screen for a phone held in one hand; desktop is the enhancement. Two-column product grid on mobile, thumb-reachable CTAs, slide-in drawers, sticky bottom action bars. Touch gestures are first-class (see GESTURES section).
2. **Frontend only.** Build the complete visual UI for everything, but payments (Razorpay), authentication, and database writes are **clearly marked placeholder functions only**. Keep payment/inventory/auth logic isolated in separate files so real backends can be wired in later without touching visual components. Prepare structure for Supabase but use local placeholder data now.
3. **Single theme config.** ALL colors, fonts, brand name, copy strings, easter-egg codes, drop timer target, and Instagram handle live in one `theme.config.ts` / `constants.ts` so the entire site can be re-themed for a future brand by editing one file.
4. **NO RETURNS — final sale brand.** Never mention returns, refunds, exchanges, or "7-day policies" anywhere. Frame it romantically: *"Every piece is one of a kind. Once it finds its person, it's theirs forever."*
5. **Performance budget.** No real video files. Achieve cinema with lightweight layered animation (CSS/canvas particles, gradient glows, subtle transforms). Lazy-load below the fold. Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` — all particles and shimmer gracefully disable.

---

## MUST NOT LOOK AI-GENERATED — HUMAN CRAFT RULES

This site must feel hand-assembled by a person who loves old things, not generated by a machine. Enforce these:

- **Imperfection by design:** elements slightly rotated (0.5°–2°), overlapping like a real scrapbook collage, torn and deckled paper edges that are irregular, tape and paper-clip details, uneven wax seals.
- **Real textures, not gradients:** scanned-paper grain, film grain, soft vignettes, fabric and pressed-flower textures. Avoid perfect linear/radial gradients, plastic gloss, neon glows, and generic 3D-render look.
- **Organic layout:** asymmetric editorial composition. Not every section centered; not every card identical. Vary spacing, angles, and ornament placement so no two sections feel stamped from the same template.
- **Human typography:** real typographic care — optical sizes, tight display kerning for headings, generous body leading, true small caps for prices. Use beautiful, sophisticated typography—no generic "Canva-style" scripts. 
- **Handmade iconography:** icons as old engravings / hand-drawn line illustrations, never generic icon-pack glyphs or emoji as UI.

## DESIGN SYSTEM

**Color palette — "candlelit twilight collage":**

- Base background: deep espresso / almost black with a warm plum undertone `#1A1114`
- Darker panels / drawers / modals: near-black `#120C0F`
- Dusk-sky accent: twilight violet `#4A3556` and deep mauve `#8C5F6E`
- Primary text & headings: aged cream `#EADCC3` and warm parchment `#F3E9D7`
- Primary accent (prices, buttons, sparkle): antique gold `#C5A059`
- Glow / candlelight: warm amber / fairy-light gold `#E8B871`
- Romantic accents (florals, ribbons): dusty rose `#B0788A` and deep purple `#4B3645`
- Warm wood / leather: dark aged brown `#4A332A`

**Typography (Premium & Sophisticated):**
- **Display/Headings:** Bodoni Moda or Cinzel Decorative (high-end, sharp, editorial serif).
- **Body:** EB Garamond or Lora.
- **Lore cards & poetry:** Special Elite or a realistic vintage typewriter font.
- **Handwritten accents:** Pinyon Script or Tangerine (elegant, authentic cursive, strictly used for short annotations like "I wish I met me in another life. ♡").
- **Casing:** Enforce proper title casing for elegance (avoid the all-lowercase Gen-Z trend).

## HIDDEN LIFE — IDLE & AMBIENT ANIMATIONS

If the user is **idle for ~45 seconds**, the page quietly comes alive:
- the candle flame flickers a little more
- the fairy-light jar glows brighter for a moment
- the crescent moon in the window brightens
- a purple butterfly flutters past
- dust drifts softly in the amber light

Any interaction returns everything to calm.
